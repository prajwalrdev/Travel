Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a taxi?

You can book a taxi through our website by visiting the booking page, through our mobile app, or by calling our customer service number. Simply provide your pickup location, destination, date, time, and vehicle preference to complete your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), digital wallets (PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay), and cash payments directly to the driver. For corporate clients, we also offer invoice-based payments.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking through our website or app. Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the time of cancellation relative to your scheduled pickup time:

  • More than 24 hours before pickup: Full refund
  • 12-24 hours before pickup: 75% refund
  • 4-12 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 4 hours before pickup: No refund
What if my flight is delayed?

For airport pickups, we monitor flight arrivals in real-time. If your flight is delayed, your driver will adjust the pickup time accordingly at no extra charge. Our system automatically tracks flight information based on the flight number you provide during booking.

Do you offer child seats?

Yes, we offer child seats for different age groups. You can request a child seat during the booking process for an additional fee. We have infant carriers (0-12 months), toddler seats (1-4 years), and booster seats (4-12 years) available. Please request these in advance to ensure availability.

What areas do you service?

We provide taxi and car rental services throughout the city and surrounding areas. Our outstation services cover major destinations within a 500-mile radius. For specific inquiries about service availability in your area, please contact our customer support team.
